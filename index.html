Our Series of Gamified Assessments

Sample our game-based assessments

Magic Square

Candidate must pick the number from the bottom and arrange them according to their arithmetic operation. and arrange correctly to avoid penalty

Typical Time to complete : 6 Minutes

facets: spatial scanning, visual memory, flexibility of closure.

Tower Block

Stack up the blocks as high as you can. Stack game refers to a variety of gameplay strategies, techniques, or insights that help players perform better

Approx Time to complete : 2 Minutes

facets: Timing and Precision, Focus and Concentration, visual memory, flexibility of closure.

Number Sum Game

Candidates must check which numbers are falling and collect those that sum up to the target number. Be careful to avoid the others.

Typical Time to complete : 3 Minutes

facets:memory assessment, perceptual speed, memory span.

Link Swipe

Candidates are presented with a pair of words and must swipe to indicate whether the pair of words are approximate synonyms, antonyms, or neither.

Typical Time to complete : 6 Minutes

facets: lexical knowledge, processing verbal information, grammatical sensitivity.

Sorting Quiz

Questions on Sorting Algorithm are there. Have to select correct answer, if wrong penalty of 1 marks will be there, Also have time limit.

Typical Time to complete : 30-40 seconds

facets: memory span, knowledge of sorting Algorithm ie: Bubble sort, Insertion sort

Reasoning and Aptitude quiz

Here there are different categories of quiz, User have there choice on selecting the quiz on the basis of time, topic and no. of ques.

Typical Time to complete : 2 Minutes

facets: quantitative reasoning, perceptual speed, memory span.
